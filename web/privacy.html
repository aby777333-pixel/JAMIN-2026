Privacy Policy

JAMIN Properties · Last updated:

This policy explains what information the JAMIN Properties mobile app and website (“JAMIN”, “we”, “us”) collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using JAMIN you agree to this policy.

1. Information we collect

  • Account & profile: name, email address, phone number, profile photo, and your role in the network.
  • Identity (KYC): identity-verification details you submit (e.g. ID type and number) for compliance, where applicable.
  • Activity: property enquiries, bookings, wishlists, leads, referrals, commissions and wallet/withdrawal records you create in the app.
  • Device permissions you grant: camera (to capture property photos for ads and brochures), location (to geo-verify on-site photos and site visits), photo library (to save and pick images), and a push notification token.
  • Technical: device type, app version, and basic diagnostics.
  • Website: if you register on our site, the email, phone and name you provide.

2. How we use your information

  • To create and operate your account, hierarchy placement and referral attribution.
  • To provide core features: discovery, calculators, bookings, commissions, wallet, marketing tools, leaderboards and notifications.
  • To send transactional notifications (commission credited, withdrawal status, KYC status, new leads, milestones) by in-app message and push.
  • To verify identity and prevent fraud and abuse.
  • To generate marketing copy with AI when you use the AI tools (see §4).

3. How information is stored & shared

We do not sell your personal information. Data is stored with our infrastructure provider Supabase (Postgres database, authentication and storage) acting as our processor. Access is restricted by row-level security so you (and authorised administrators) see only what you are permitted to. We share data only with service providers needed to run the app, or where required by law.

4. AI features

When you use the AI tools (listing copy, social posts, lead scoring, assistant), the text and context you submit are processed by Anthropic (Claude) via a secure server function to generate a response. Do not enter sensitive personal data into AI prompts. We log generated results to your account so you can re-use them.

5. Your choices & rights

  • Update your profile in the app at any time.
  • Control notifications under Settings → Notifications.
  • Request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by emailing us (see §8). Note that some financial records are retained for audit and legal compliance.

6. Data retention

We keep personal data for as long as your account is active and as needed to provide the service. Financial and audit records (commission ledger, transactions) are retained as required for accounting and legal compliance even after account closure.

7. Children

JAMIN is intended for users aged 18 and over and is not directed at children.

8. Contact

Questions or data requests: privacy@jaminproperties.co. We respond within a reasonable time.

9. Changes

We may update this policy; material changes will be reflected by the “last updated” date above.
